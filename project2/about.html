Reasons for This Website's Creation

Hello and welcome to my website containing information on first-aid procedures. The reason behind the creation of this website is to better inform you and others to help be the hero that'll be needed in the case that disaster strikes.

You don't need to have any superpowers like the heroes in the movies. Though having the knowledge of human anatomy and physiology will help out in your endeavors to help others. Dr. Strange would know what I'm talking about, right?

Regardless, through the techniques provided on this website, you will get to know some parts of the human body. Also, do take into account that an adult's body has organs in different locations in comparison to those of children's and that everyone's body should be treated as their own separate cases. You never know if someone could be born with some kind of genetic mutation like osteogenesis imperfecta which would be a rather... unpleasant situation if said person went into cardiac arrest and required CPR.
